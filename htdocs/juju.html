My Coin Collection

Take a look around my coin collection!

1. 2017 Proof Set

Proof sets are coins made with a special process by the mint. They have mirror like fields and frosty devices which gives them a cool look.

I got 2 2017 Proof Sets as birthday gifts! They include all of the denominations of the coins from that year, and one also the America the Beautiful quarters too.

2017 Regular Proof Set

2017 Birthday Proof Set

2. Sample Slabbed Coins

Sometimes at coin conventions, coin grading companies will hand out slabbed coins(coins in a holder) as samples.

I managed to get a few at the American Numismatic Association's World's Fair of Money.

These are actually quite valuable because there are some people who like to collect sample slabs, and will pay a lot for them!

3. American Silver Eagle Proof

5. (Mostly)Uncirculated Lincoln Cents

This is my collection of older (mostly)uncirculated cents. I've got two Memorial Cents, and 3 Wheat Cents, all in uncirculated condition!

These are my favorite coins in uncirculated conditions because I like the red shine that they give off.

I found some while coin roll hunting, and others I got as gifts or prizes.
